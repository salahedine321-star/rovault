Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

RoVault is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

RoVault collects minimal information to provide our services:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous data about how you use the app
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version (collected by AdMob)
  • Advertising ID: Used by Google AdMob to show relevant ads
  • Local Storage: Streak, coins, scores stored locally on your device

We do NOT collect: names, emails, phone numbers, location, or any personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use Information

  • To display advertisements via Google AdMob
  • To provide and improve the App's features
  • To analyze anonymous usage patterns

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — Displays advertisements. Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store — App distribution. Apple Privacy Policy

4. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in Settings → Privacy → Tracking.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

RoVault is rated 12+ and is NOT directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us at salahedine321@gmail.com.

6. GDPR — European Users

If you are in the EEA, you have rights regarding your data including access, deletion, and opt-out of personalized advertising. Contact us at salahedine321@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

7. Data Storage & Security

All user data is stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults. We do not store personal data on external servers.

8. Your Choices

  • Opt out of personalized ads: Settings → Privacy → Tracking
  • Delete all data: Delete the app from your device
  • Disable notifications: Settings → Notifications → RoVault
